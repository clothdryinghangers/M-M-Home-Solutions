# Image Size Guidelines

## Standardized Image Sizes

### Hero Slider Images
- **Size**: 1200x600 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Location**: `public/images/hero/`
- **File Size**: Under 500KB recommended
- **Files**:
  - ceiling-cloth-drying-hangers.jpg
  - wall-mounted-cloth-hangers.jpg
  - invisible-grills.jpg
  - pigeon-safety-nets.jpg
  - bamboo-roller-blinds.jpg
  - shoe-racks.jpg
  - anti-bird-spikes.jpg
  - mosquito-nets.jpg

### Category Images
- **Size**: 1200x600 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Usage**: Category hero banners
- **File Size**: Under 500KB recommended

### Product Images
- **Size**: 400x300 pixels (4:3 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Usage**: Product cards and listings
- **File Size**: Under 200KB recommended

### Services Grid Images
- **Size**: 400x300 pixels (4:3 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Usage**: Services grid on homepage
- **File Size**: Under 200KB recommended

## Notes
- All images should be optimized for web
- Use consistent aspect ratios for better visual consistency
- Compress images before uploading to reduce load times
